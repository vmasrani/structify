## Tech Giant Announces New AI Research Lab in Seattle

**By:** Rachel Kim, Tech Reporter
**Published:** October 21, 2024 - 11:45 AM EST
**Location:** Seattle, WA

MegaTech Corp announced today the opening of a new $500 million AI research facility in downtown Seattle, creating an estimated 1,200 jobs over the next three years.

The 200,000 square-foot facility will focus on natural language processing and computer vision applications. Dr. Andrew Chen, formerly of MIT, will lead the research division.

"This investment represents our commitment to advancing AI technology responsibly," said CEO Maria Gonzalez during the opening ceremony.

Local officials praised the development, noting its potential economic impact on the region's tech ecosystem.
