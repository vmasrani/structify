# Research Notes

**Study:** Impact of Remote Work on Software Developer Productivity
**Researcher:** Dr. Patricia Wong
**Date:** October 2024
**Institution:** Stanford University

## Key Findings

Conducted survey of 500 software developers across 50 companies (June-September 2024).

### Productivity Metrics

- 68% reported same or higher productivity when remote
- Average coding output: +15% compared to office baseline
- Meeting time reduced by 22% on average
- Context switching decreased significantly

### Challenges Identified

- Communication delays (mentioned by 45% of respondents)
- Difficulty onboarding new team members (38%)
- Reduced spontaneous collaboration (52%)

### Recommendations

1. Implement async-first communication protocols
2. Schedule focused "no-meeting" blocks
3. Invest in better collaboration tools
4. Maintain regular team social events

**Next Steps:** Follow-up study in 6 months to track long-term trends.
